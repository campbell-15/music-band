Biography & Awards

The Judgement Day is a rock band comprised of Dom Mysterio on vocals, Fin Balor on guitar, Rhea Ripley on bass, and Damien Priest on drums. Known for their powerful performances and diverse musical influences, they deliver hard-hitting rock with emotionally charged lyrics. Their albums, including "Eclipse of Souls" and "Reign of Fire," have gained critical acclaim and a devoted fanbase. With their dynamic stage presence and captivating sound, The Judgement Day continues to make their mark in the music industry
